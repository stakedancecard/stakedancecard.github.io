Stake Dance Card App

This app may be used to gain admittance to LDS stake dances in participating stakes. The app must be signed by a parent and bishop before it is activated.

iPhone and iPad

Android phones & tablets

Dance Card

Once the youth profile information is added, including name, photo, ward, stake, and parent info, a bishopric member interviews the youth, then signs the app with the appropriate password. The app may then be used for admittance to dances.

Calendar and Standards

A calendar of stake dances is included along with maps to the dance locations. Information on stake dress and behavior standards is also included.

Sharing

Share dance information using Facebook, Twitter, or email.
